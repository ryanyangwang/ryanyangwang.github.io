Biography

I am a Ph. D. candidate majoring in Mass Communications and minoring in Social Data Analytics. My main research interests are two-folded: (1) how digital infrastructure influences political polarization within the U.S. context; (2) how social media facilitates the formation and dissemination of public opinion, and how digital trace data could be utilized to better understand today’s political behavior. Additionally, I am interested in incorporating computational methods in my own research, including natural language processing, network analysis, agent-based modeling, and etc. In my spare time, I am also a hip-hop beat-maker and producer.
